My name is Ho-Zhen Chen. I am a PhD student in the Civil and Environmental Engineering Department at UMass Amherst. I am part of the Fluids@UMass group under the supervision of Colin Gleason. I got my undergraduate degree from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and my masters from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.

My research focuses on arctic river morphology. More specifically, time series changes in river width and its relationship to hydraulic geometrv. In short, I want to design the stream flow data center for all arctic rivers.
