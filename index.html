Hello my name is Ho-Zhen Chen. This website is hosted by GitHub and the html background was grabbed from the Zurb (http://zurb.com/)

Here at the University of Michigan I worked in the Real-Time Water Systems Lab under the supervision of Prof. Kerkez. Link to the lab website can be found upstairs at the 1061 tab, which is the GGBL room number of the lab.

Previously I've been working on sensor housing design. This year, under the supervision of Prof. Kerkez, I'll be setting up a sensor network at the University's Bio-Station.
